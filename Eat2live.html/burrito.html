Burrito

Better Than Chipotle

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups White Rice

  • 4 Chicken Thighs

  • 1 Can Black Beans

  • 1 Can of Tomatoes and Chilis

  • Low Fat Sour Cream

  • Small Onion

  • Low fat Shredded Cheese

Preparation

Season the chicken and fry in olive oil then dice it. Dice the small onion. Boil 2 cups of rice. Boil the beans and season. Simple

Action

First, pour the rice in the bowl. Next, add the beans and tomatoes with chilis. Drop in the chicken and top with sour cream and diced onions We promise it's better than Chipotle, and cheaper too.
