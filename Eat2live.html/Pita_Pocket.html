Pita Pocket

Even healthier than it is easy




Ingredients

  • Mini Pita

  • Caesar Dressing

  • Grilled Chicken Breast

  • Lettuce

  • Parmesan Cheese

Preparation

Basically all you have to do to prep this dish is chop some lettuce, and season some chicken breast.


Action

First, put a little oil on a skillet, and fry your seasoned chicken breast. When it's done, dice it up. Cut your Pita's in half and pry them open to make pockets. Now place in your chicken, lettuce, parmesan, and caesar dressing. This dish is even healtheir than it is easy.
