Ingredients

  • Pork Tenderloin

  • Spices (Salt, pepper, garlic powder, oregano, smoked paprika)

  • balsamic vinegar

  • olive oil

  • Sourdough Bread

  • Cab Sav Reduction

  • Stock of your choice

  • Dijon mustard

Preperation

Make a marinade of the listed seasonings with the vinegar and olive oil. Put the pork in a freezer bag filled with the mariande and let it sit for a day or 2.

Action

Sear the pork on a cast iron pan, then place in the oven covered at 400 degrees for 20 minutes. Check the temperature for 145. If it's there or close to it, take it out and let it rest on the cutting board for 10 minutes. Pour half the bottle of wine and some stock of your choice into a pot and reduce. When its about 1/4th of what it was, lower the heat, add butter and mustard and cook down until it coats the back of a spoon. Toast the sourdough slices in the same cast iron you seared the pork in. Dip the pork in the sauce and place on the bread along with a slice of Munster Cheese. Eat while it's hot.
