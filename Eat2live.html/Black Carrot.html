Black Carrot Stew

"Smokey Soup for the Soul"




Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs

  • Chicken broth

  • Onions

  • Garlic

  • Bacon Ends

  • Tomato Paste

  • Red and Green Peppers

  • Black, White, and Orange Carrots

Preparation

First you'll want to dice up your aromatics (Onions, garlic, and peppers) all except for the carrots, which you will instead, chop into even pieces. you'll season the chicken simply with black pepper and salt. very simple Preparation.


Action

Heat up a stainless steel pot and place a tablespoon of oil in. After it gets nice and hot, add in the bacon ends. As the fat renders from the bacon ends, add in the chicken thighs skin side down. Let the chicken brown. When both the bacon ends and chicken have browned, remove them from the pot. Next, add in the onions and peppers, using their water to deglaze the fond from the bottom of the pan. You must take care that the fond doesn't burn, and in a pinch, you may add a little water and scrap at it with a wooden spoon. Gently cook the onions and peppers for about 10 minutes letting them carmalize. When it's time, add in your diced garlic. Let the garlic cook for about 30 seconds, then add a couple teaspoons of tomato paste and add the chicken back in. Stir everything and get it all acquainted, frying out the raw tomato paste flavor. Now it's time to add in our chicken broth. Bring it up to a boil then let it simmer. Season it all with salt pepper and garlic and it should be done in about two hours. Such an easy smokey soup for the soul.
