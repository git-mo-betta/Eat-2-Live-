"Wawa" Buffalo Chicken Wrap

"You'll never believe it was only 350 calories."

Ingredients

  • Low Calories wraps like "Carb Balance"

  • Chicken Breast or thighs (boneless)

  • Low calorie buffalo sauce of your choosing

  • Crisp lettuce recommended but baby spinach will do

  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder

Preparation

First, preheat your oven to 375 degrees. Season the chicken with the salt, pepper, and the garlic powder then sit it to the side. This time will allow the seasoning to penetrate while simultainously allowing it to get closer to room temperature for more even cooking. In the meantime, chop the letteuce to desireable sizes. Prepare a nonstick pan for your wrap to toast. After 30 minutes of waiting, sit your chicken in a lightly oiled roasting pan and place the pan into the preheated oven. Let roast for 45 minutes.

Action

Take the chicken out of the pan and let it rest on a cutting board. Heat up your non stick and gently toast your wrap for 30 seconds. Take the wrap off of the heat and place it on a dish to prepare your wrap. Dice the chicken. Pour a small amount of buffalo sauce on the wrap. Add the chicken, then add the lettuce to the party. Roll up the wrap, and your delicious buffalo wrap is prepared for launch. After you eat it, you'll never believe it was only 350 caloires.
