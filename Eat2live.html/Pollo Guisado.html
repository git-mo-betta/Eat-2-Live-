Pollo Guisado

"Your whole house can enjoy the aroma"




Ingredients

  • Chicken legs and/or Wings

  • Chicken Broth

  • Potatos

  • Spanish Olives

  • Seasonings (Adobo, Sazon, Goya Ham bullion, Sofrito)

  • Tomato Sauce

  • Garlic

  • Olive Oil

Preparation

Season Chicken with Adobo, Dice the garlic, and peel and cut the potatoes. Heat the pan and pour in a tablespoon or two of olive oil.


Action

Saut√© the chicken, take it out and sit it on the side. Deglaze the fond of the pan with the Sofrito and garlic. After about two minutes of that its time to pour in a small can of tomato sauce. While thats heating up, season it with the Sazon and the Ham flavoring. At this point you can add the chicken back in, and pour in the broth. Add in the potatoes and the olives. Bring it to the boil and let it simmer. 2 hours later it will be done, and your whole house can enjoy the aroma.
