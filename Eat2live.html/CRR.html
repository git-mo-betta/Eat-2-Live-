Chuletas, Red Beans, Rice

"Just like grandma used to make it"

Ingredients

  • Thin cut Porkchops

  • Can of Kidney Beans

  • White Rice

  • Seasonings (Salt, Pepper, Adobo, Sazon, Goya Ham flavor,

  • Aromatics (Shallots and Garlic)

Preparation

To prep, you'll want to season the porkchops with salt, pepper, and Adobo. Wash the rice to get the starch out. Saut√© the aromatics in a small pot, add the tomato sauce sazon and ham flavoring. Pour in the beans and let them simmer.

Action

Go ahead and make 2 or 3 cups of white rice. Heat up a pan with about an inch of canola oil in it. Let the oil get hot and drop in the porkchops. Flip them every 3 minutes. They should be done in about 10 minutes. Just like grandma used to make it.
