Ingredients

  • Yukon Gold Potatoes

  • Spices (salt, pepper, sazon, chives, garlic powder)

  • butter

  • Chedder Cheese

  • Onions

  • Olive oil

Preperation

Dice the onions, shred the chedder and skin the potatoes. Chop the potatoes to your liking and place in a pot of salted and boiling water for 7 minutes.

Action

Oil a cast iron and start to carmelize the onions. Add the potatoes halfway through the carmelization process. Add all the seasonings during this time except for the chives. Flip occasionally until potatoes soften. Turn down the heat, add the cheese, and finish with chives.
