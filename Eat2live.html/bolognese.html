Ingredients

  • mirepoix

  • bottle of Cab Sav

  • Ground Beef

  • Spices (Salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, oregano, bay leaf, nutmeg)

  • Heavy Cream

  • Fresh Garlic

  • Chicken stock

  • Balsamic Vinegar

Preperation

First dice the mirepoix. Oil a pot or dutch oven and brown the beef. Take it out and sweat the mirepoix for 20 minutes. Add fresh diced garlic and the beef back in. Add tomato paste, and cook for 2 minutes before pouring in a bottle of wine and some chicken stock.

Action

Season the mix, and add in a bay leaf or 2. After it cooks for about 3 hours, open it up and pour in some balsamic vinegar. After it boils out, turn off the heat. After 5 minutes, add in a tablespoon of butter and stir. Shave in some nutmeg, and then pour in some heavy cream until it's a nice bright orange. Stir in the cream while it's on low heat and taste for seasoning. If it tastes amazing then you did it right.
