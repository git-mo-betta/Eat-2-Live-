PR Jambalaya

You aren't in Lousiana, you're in Puerto Rico




Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Rice

  • Seasonings (Sazon, adobo, Goya ham flavor,

  • Spanish Olives

  • Bacon Ends

  • Kielbasa or any Smoked Sausage

  • Sofrito

  • Aromatics, Garlic Cloves, Red Peppers, Green peppers, Onions

  • Olive Oil

  • Small Can Tomato Sauce

  • Chicken bullion

Preparation

First off, dice up the aromatics very small. Sear the Sausage and bacon ends then remove them from heat. Sweat the aromatics for 15 minutes, then add the garlic and sofrito. After a minute or so add the tomato sauce and the seasonings. Cook the tomato mixture for a couple minutes then add the meat back in.


Action

Now you can add in 3 cups of water and the bullion cube. Add your rinsed rice to the pot and let the water boil. As the water gets lower, turn the heat down and close the top. Turn the rice every 7 minutes for about an hour. The rice should be sticky and soft. Take one bite and acknowledge that you aren't in Lousiana, you're in Puerto Rico
